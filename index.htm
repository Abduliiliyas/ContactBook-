Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

Tally counter (the "App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information Collection and Use

Tally Pro is designed as a "Privacy-First" utility. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, identifiable data, or usage analytics from your device.

2. Local Data Storage

The App uses your device's local storage (AsyncStorage) to save your current counter value. This data stays 100% on your device and is never uploaded to any external servers or third parties. If you uninstall the app, this locally stored count is typically deleted by the operating system.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not use any third-party SDKs, advertising networks, or tracking tools that collect your data. There are no hidden background processes monitoring your activity.

4. Permissions

This App requires no special permissions (such as Camera, Location, or Contacts) to function. It operates entirely offline.

5. Children’s Privacy

Since we do not collect any data, our App is safe for users of all ages, including children. We do not knowingly or unknowingly collect information from anyone.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

📒 Contact Book

Privacy Policy · Last updated: April 1, 2026

Your contacts, your control. This privacy policy explains how the Contact Book app handles your data. We believe in complete transparency and have designed the app to respect your privacy above all else.

📌 Data Collection – None

Zero telemetry No servers No analytics

Contact Book does NOT collect, transmit, or store any personal information on external servers. The app works entirely offline on your device. We never receive your contacts, name, phone number, email, address, or any other data.

✅ No registration, no cloud sync, no tracking. Everything you add stays only on your phone unless you explicitly choose to export it (as a CSV file) or share it with another app.

🔐 What data is stored locally?

The app stores the contact information you voluntarily enter:

  • Full name (required)
  • Phone number (required)
  • Email address (optional)
  • Physical address (optional)

This data is saved exclusively on your device using AsyncStorage (React Native’s persistent key-value storage). No data ever leaves your phone unless you initiate an export or share action.

📁 Export & sharing features

Contact Book includes an optional "Export to CSV" button. When you tap it:

  • A CSV file containing your contacts is generated inside a temporary app folder.
  • Your device’s system share sheet opens, allowing you to save, email, or send the file to another app.
  • The file is not uploaded anywhere automatically. You control where it goes.

After sharing, the temporary file remains on your device until it is overwritten the next time you export. No external service receives this data without your explicit action.

👶 Children’s privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since all data stays on the user’s device and is never transmitted, there is no risk of data exposure through our systems. Parents or guardians supervise the device usage as they see fit.

🔒 Security

Because the app is completely offline, the security of your contact data depends on your device’s built-in protections (screen lock, encryption, etc.). We do not have any access to your information. For additional safety, we recommend keeping your device’s operating system up to date.

📱 Permissions used

The app does not require internet access, camera, microphone, or location permissions. The only optional system feature used is file sharing (to export CSV), which triggers the native Android/iOS share dialog. No extra background permissions are requested.

⚖️ Changes to this policy

If we ever update this privacy page, we will post the revised version here. Because the app does not collect user data, changes will only clarify or improve transparency. The “last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect any modifications.

📧 Contact us
